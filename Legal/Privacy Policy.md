**Effective Date**: November 1st, 2025

Foundation respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

## Information We Collect

We collect your name, phone number, and email when you sign up or register for an event. This information helps us manage your place in line and contact you with event-related updates.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to:

- Contact you about your place in line or event participation
- Communicate essential event updates or logistical information

We do **NOT** sell, share, or use your information for marketing or promotional purposes.

## Data Storage and Retention

Your information is stored securely and is only accessible to authorized event staff. We delete all personal data **within 7 days after the event** unless required by law to keep it longer.

## Sharing Your Information

We do **NOT** sell, rent, or trade your information to anyone. If we use third-party services (such as a form or messaging platform) to collect or send information, those services may have their own privacy policies that govern how they handle your data.

## Your Rights

You may:

- Request to see what data we have about you
- Ask us to correct or delete your information at any time

To make a request, contact us at [FoundationHauntedHouse@gmail.com](mailto:FoundationHauntedHouse@gmail.com).

## Contact Us

If you have any questions or privacy concerns, please contact us at:

📧 [FoundationHauntedHouse@gmail.com](mailto:FoundationHauntedHouse@gmail.com)
